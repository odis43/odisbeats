Origins

Garageband

When my family moved into our new home, I didn't have many things to do aside from going outside and making crappy beats on my phone. I realized that I actually had a lot of fun making music, so I thought of maybe moving to a different platform.

FL Studio

After that summer, I stumbled across fruity loops, a proffesional DAW used by many artists around the world. I assumed all the basics would be the same as garageband so I gave the trial version a go.

Boy was I wrong.

It was quite the learning experience, moving from a platform that didn't emphasize mixing and mastering. After a while though, I loved making music on FL 12 and made it my main source of creating tracks.

Guitar

This was the instrument where my love for music began. I started playing when I was 11 and haven't stopped since. Every now and then when I attempt tracking, the guitar is where I go for inspiration.

I couldn't have asked for a better instrument.
